Personal Information

English Vocabulary

Personal information questions are the basis for any conversation in English and so are important for when you meet somebody for the first time. They are the building blocks from which the rest of the conversation can develop.

Some common questions and a correct form for the answer are as follows:
Notice that we are giving our answers in complete sentences to practice using them though normally we only give short answers.

Names

What is your name?

My name is Shreeya Maskey

What is your surname / last name?
My surname / last name is Maskey. 

Do you have a nickname?
        No, I don't.

Work & Occupation

What do you do?
I am a student.

Marital Status

Are you married / single?
No I'm not.

Do you have a boyfriend / girlfriend?
No, I don't.

Family

How many brothers and sister do you have?
I have no brothers and one sister.

Age

How old are you?
I am 18 years old.

When is your birthday?
My birthday is on the 8th of August.

Where were you born?
I was born in Dharan.

Contact Information

Where are you from?
I am from Kathmandu.

Do you live with your parents?
Yes, I do.

Do you live alone?
No, I don't.

Do you live in a house or an apartment?
I live in a house.

Free time activities

What do you do in your free time?
In my free time I watch youtube.

What are your hobbies?
My hobbies are dancing, reading and listening to music.

What type of music do you like?
I like Indie music.

Do you have a favourite singer or group?
My favourite singer / group is Mickey Darling, Little Mix and Mxmtoon.

What types of movies do you like?
I like adventure movies.

Do you like to read?
Yes, I do.

What do you like to read?
I have no specifications on what I like to read.

Habits

Do you have any bad habits?
Yes, one of my bad habits is procrastinating.

Do you snore?
No, I don't.

Do you smoke?
No, I don't.

Other Personal Information

What is your favourite food?
My favourite food is chicken chilly.

Is there any food that you do not like?
I like all food.

What is your favourite colour?
My favourite colour is blue.

Why do you want to learn English?
I want to learn English because it is important.

What languages do you speak?
I speak Nepali, English, Hindi and Spanish.

Do you have any pets?
No, I don't.

Which sports do you like?
The sport I like is Table Tenis.

Do you have a favourite team?
No I don't.

What is something you do well?
I am good at dancing and studying.

What is something you do not do well?
I am bad at singing and cooking.

Do you have any tattoos?
No, I don't.

Describe yourself using only two words.
I am energetic and positive.

What makes you happy?
I am happy when I watch YouTube.

Are you a good cook/singer/driver?
No, I'm not.

The structure of the question may be very different to your own native languages but there are rules concerning the structure of sentences in English.

For more information regarding the correct use of the question words such as What, Where, How and When refer to our student notes on Question Words.

For more information regarding the correct us of do/does in questions refer to our student notes on Do vs. Does.

With just a little practice you can become very good at asking and answering these basic questions with any native English speaker. Try them the next time you meet an English speaker and you may make a new friend.
